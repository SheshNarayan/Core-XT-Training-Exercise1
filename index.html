Knowledge Campus is an Indian college with campuses in Durgapur and Kolkata. It is affiliated to the West Bengal University of Technology.

The Durgapur campus comprises the NSHM School of Hotel Management, NSHM College of Management and Technology, NSHM Business School and the NSHM Faculty of Engineering & Technology.

The Kolkata Campus has the NSHM College of Management and Technology, NSHM College of Pharmaceutical Technology, NSHM Institute of Media and Design and an integrated NSHM Business School offering MBA programmes.

The dynamic Kolkata Campus has Departments of Computing, Basic and Applied Sciences, Allied Health Sciences, Engineering and Technology, Media, Design and Communication, Hospitality and Tourism and Business and Management offering world-class MBA programmes. With the changing times, we have taken growth and innovation along our stride to address industry demands. Our affiliation with leading international institutes as knowledge partners enables students to access a rich pool of resources for receiving the best of what they need by the end of their course. Moreover, we have embraced academics from a variety of disciplines, enabling us to produce curricula that teach the students to deal with the big issues enterprises are facing today from different perspectives to make each challenge easy for them.
